How it works

Autonomous from inbox
to ledger

No bots to configure. No templates to maintain. Your agentic OS handles every step — you just receive clean, structured data.

1

Your client sends a PDF

Clients email bank statements to your dedicated processing address. The agentic OS detects the document, validates it, and begins orchestration instantly — no portal logins, no manual triggers.

2

Agents extract, validate & categorise

Specialised AI agents read every transaction, parse dates and amounts, and categorise each entry against your chart of accounts — completed autonomously in under 60 seconds with 95%+ accuracy and built-in quality gates.

3

Structured Excel, zero manual effort

A ledger-ready Excel file appears in your Acctos AI dashboard. The OS can push it downstream into your bookkeeping software, notify your team, or trigger further agentic workflows — automatically.
